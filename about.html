    Our Story!

    The journey of African Market.

    Africa market was found in November 2020 and has been supplying food stuff all over Lithuania and the Baltics since then. Firstly, as distributors of dried and smoked fish, and the company is managed by a dedicated team with extensive experience in the food industry and has maintained an excellent reputation in the industry over many years. Our aim is to make top quality African and Caribbean foods and drinks accessible and affordable to consumers in and around Lithuania, Baltic and in the EU

    Our shop locations are central with easy access by public transport. The nearest bus stations are zalgirio street, the area and from neighboring boroughs. All are easily accessible by car from gym plus or Jammi kebab with free parking available for shoppers.

    Our mission

    Quality product with exceptional price-value.

    High-Quality Products

    We offer authentic African products, carefully sourced from trusted suppliers to ensure freshness, quality, and authenticity. Every item is selected with care to bring you the best of Africa, right here in Lithuania .

    Affordable Prices

    Our goal is to make African products accessible to everyone at competitive prices. We work closely with our suppliers to keep costs low while maintaining high quality.

    Fast & Reliable Delivery

    We prioritize efficiency, ensuring that your orders are processed quickly and delivered on time. Whether you’re ordering food, skincare, or seafood, we guarantee prompt and secure shipping.
