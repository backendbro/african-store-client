Can i order over the phone?

No, we currently only accept orders through our website to ensure accuracy and secure transactions. However, if you need assistance, our customer support team is available to guide you through the process.

I am having difficulty placing an order?

If you experience any issues while placing an order, please try the following:

  1. Refresh the page or clear your browser cache.
  2. Ensure you have a stable internet connection
  3. Try using a different browser or device

What payment methods does accept?

We accept multiple secure payment methods, including:

  1. Apple Pay
  2. Google Pay
  3. Debit and Credit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express)
  4. Other payment options may be available at checkout.

Can i amend my order once placed?

Once an order is placed, it is processed immediately to ensure prompt delivery. While we strive to accommodate requests, modifications may not always be possible. If you need to make changes, please contact us as soon as possible.

How do i know if my order was successful?

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation email containing your order details and payment receipt. If you do not receive this email within a few minutes, please check your spam/junk folder or contact our support team.

What if my order is incorrect?

If you receive an incorrect item, please contact us within 24 hour of delivery with your order number and photos of the received items. We will review your case and arrange for a replacement or refund where applicable.

How do I search for a product?

You can search for products using the search bar at the top of our website at shop or category options. Simply enter keywords related to the product, and relevant results will be displayed.

Do you offer bulk purchases or wholesale pricing?

Yes, we offer bulk and wholesale pricing for select products. If you’re interested in making a bulk purchase, please contact our sales team for more details.

How do I know if an item is in stock?

All product pages display real time stock availability. If an item is out of stock, you may have the option to sign up for restock notifications, by subscribing to our newsletter

Can I save items in my cart for later?

Yes, you can add items to your cart and they will remain there for a limited period. However, we recommend creating an account to save your cart and wishlist permanently.

Do you offer gift packaging or special requests?

Currently, we do not offer gift packaging, but we are working on introducing this feature. However, If you have special requests, please contact our customer service before placing your order.

What currencies do you accept?

We accept EUR (€) as the primary currency. Your bank may apply conversion rates if using a different currency.

Is my payment information secure?

Yes, we use industry standard encryption and security measures to protect your payment information. All transactions are processed through secure payment gateways.

Do you offer “Buy Now, Pay Later” options?

At this time, we do not offer “Buy Now, Pay Later” services. However, we may introduce this option in the future.

4. Can I pay on delivery?

No, all orders must be prepaid before shipping. We do not offer cash on delivery. Though very soon we will introduce that soon.

What should I do if my payment is declined?

If your payment is declined, please:

  1. Check that your card details are correct.
  2. Ensure your bank has not blocked the transaction.
  3. Try using a different payment method.
If the issue persists, contact your bank or our support team for assistance.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 7 days of delivery, provided the items are unused and in their original packaging. Some perishable goods may not be eligible for returns.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, contact our support team with your order details and reason for return. Approved refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days.

How do I cancel an order?

Orders can only be canceled if they have not yet been processed for shipping. Contact us as soon as possible if you need to cancel an order.

What if my item is damaged on arrival?

If you receive a damaged item, please send us clear photos within 24 hours of delivery. We will review the case and arrange for a replacement or refund.

How long does it take to process a refund?

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. The time it takes to reflect in your account depends on your payment provider.

Do I need an account to place an order?

No, you can check out as a guest. However, creating an account allows you to track orders, save your shipping details, and enjoy a more personalized shopping experience.

Can I track my order?

Yes, once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this to track your package in real time.

Can I pre order out-of-stock items?

Some products may be available for pre-order. If pre-orders are available, the product page will indicate the estimated restock date.

Do you have a loyalty program or discounts?

We offer seasonal promotions, discounts, and loyalty rewards for our customers. Subscribe to our newsletter to stay updated.
